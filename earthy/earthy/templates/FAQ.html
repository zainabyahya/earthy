Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is earthy, and why should we use it?

Earthy is an app used to track your plastic usage to determine wether your plastic usage is average, above or below, from there you are given ways to contribute to helping the environment locally, or on a more international level. This way you are helping make the world a safer place for people to live in, without it slowly deteriorating.

Q: Do i use this website only once, or do i have to frequently update my consumption on it?

You can update your consumption on it in order to compare your results, or simply depend on the original results.

Q: Why should we care about what is happening to the environment?

What is happening to the environment is affecting us in ways well beyond what we could imagine, and affecting our health as well as other living creatures who are endangered and on the brisk of vanishing from earth.

Q: Why is it important to reduce our plastic consumption?

Future generations will suffer way more than we are suffering simply because of our choices concerning plastic. By reducing our plastic usage, we are helping save the lives of those to come after us, and all of humanity. So for the sake of our children, let us make safe and easy decisions together, to save this beautiful earth we are given and have it for a much longer time.

Q: What was it that made you want to track your plastic consumption?

Personally, i have always been interested in making sure that i am leaving this earth satisfied in knowing that i have done my best not to leave harmful footprints, and to appreciate the earth that has been given to us. It is our job to cherish this gift we have been given, and to not destroy it simply for an easy way out. Creating this website has basically been an extra step for me towards doing something that matters, and contributing to a better future.

Q: Plastic is making our life easier, so how are we to believe it is harmful?

Right now it is making our life easier but most people aren’t noticing the true damage, and it will keep on progressing wether we acknowledge it or not, thus we must educate ourselves on what we are actually doing poisoning the world.

Q: What is going to happen if we keep on the same road?

We will start quickly suffering because of it, increasing global warming that destroys our planet leading to a state of deterioration of the human race, and all living creatures because we have no place to live, simply because we sought the easy way out.

Q: What is going to change if we change our plastic habits?

We will save the earth. As simple as it seems, it is true. Things will get better, and we will be okay if we just had an awareness to how bad things are right now.

Q: I am putting my used plastic in a pile to recycle, isn’t that enough?

Only 15% or less of recyclable material ends up actually being recycled, the rest of it ends up in landfills and the ocean, Thus we have to take other measures to help.
